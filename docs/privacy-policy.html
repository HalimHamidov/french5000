Privacy Policy — French 5000

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Overview

French 5000 is a vocabulary learning app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. The app does not require an account, does not transmit any information to external servers, and does not use analytics or advertising SDKs.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only using browser localStorage:

  • Your learning progress (which words you have studied and their review schedules)
  • App settings (words per day, reminder time)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, the app schedules a local notification on your device. No data is sent to any server to deliver this notification.

Internet Access

The app loads its vocabulary data from files bundled within the app itself. No internet connection is required or used during normal operation.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from children or any other users.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at our GitHub repository.
